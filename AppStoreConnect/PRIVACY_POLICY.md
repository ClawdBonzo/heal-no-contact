This is the privacy policy for the Heal: No Contact Recovery mobile application ("Heal", "the app", "we", "our", or "us") developed and distributed by Rob Goldstein ("the developer").

By using Heal, you agree to the terms described below.

---

## TL;DR

- Everything you type into the app — journal entries, mood check-ins, mantras, SOS logs — is stored **only on your device**.
- We do **not** collect, sell, share, or have access to any of your personal content.
- We do **not** track you across apps or websites.
- The only data that leaves your device is limited purchase information handled by Apple and our subscription provider RevenueCat (to verify you paid for premium).

---

## 1. Information Stored On Your Device

The following data is stored **locally** on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

- Your ex's name (if provided during onboarding)
- Breakup date and relationship duration (if provided)
- Your personal mantra
- Your no-contact goal (in days)
- Journal entries
- Mood check-ins
- Unsent letters
- Emergency SOS log entries
- Streak, XP, level, quest, and badge data
- Daily check-in notifications preferences

This data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework on your device. It is backed up to your iCloud only if you have enabled iCloud Backup for Heal in your device's Settings. Apple's privacy policy governs any iCloud backup.

We, the developer, **do not have access** to this data. There is no account system, no remote server, and no analytics that transmit your content.

---

## 2. Information Shared with Third Parties

Heal uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

### Apple App Store and In-App Purchases
When you make a purchase (subscription or lifetime unlock), Apple processes the transaction. We do not see your payment details. Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

### RevenueCat
We use RevenueCat to manage and verify your subscription status. When you make a purchase, RevenueCat receives a transaction receipt from Apple. This receipt contains:
- An anonymous Apple-generated user identifier
- The product you purchased
- Purchase date and expiration date

RevenueCat does **not** receive your name, email, or any content from the app. RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

### Apple Push Notifications (optional)
If you opt in to daily check-in reminders or milestone notifications, your device registers for Apple Push Notification service. No content from your journal or mood entries is sent through notifications — only generic reminders like "Time for your daily check-in."

---

## 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

- **No analytics**. We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or any similar tool.
- **No advertising SDKs**. We do not show ads and we do not have advertising identifiers.
- **No tracking**. We do not use the App Tracking Transparency framework to track you across apps or websites.
- **No account system**. We do not collect your email, phone number, or any sign-in credentials.
- **No crash reports with content**. If you opt into Apple's crash reporting at the OS level, Apple may receive anonymized crash stacks, but these contain no journal content.

---

## 4. Children's Privacy

Heal is not intended for use by children under 17. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. The app's subject matter (breakup recovery, grief processing) is rated 17+ in the App Store.

---

## 5. Your Rights

Because all your content is stored locally on your device, you are in full control of your data:

- **Access**: all your data is visible in the app.
- **Deletion**: delete any individual entry in-app, or delete the app entirely from your device to erase all local data.
- **Export**: [planned for a future version]

If you live in a region with data protection laws (EU/UK GDPR, California CCPA, etc.), note that we don't hold any of your personal content, so there is no server-side "request for deletion" to make. Uninstalling the app is the authoritative delete.

For subscription-related data (held by Apple and RevenueCat), you can exercise rights under their respective privacy policies.

---

## 6. Data Security

Because your content never leaves your device, its security depends on your device's security (screen lock, iOS encryption, iCloud Backup settings). We recommend enabling a device passcode and Face ID / Touch ID.
